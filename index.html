2020 Plant Discoveries

Amorphophallus caudatus

A new species of flowering plant found in the lowland forests of the Abasig-Matogdon-Mananap Natural Biotic Area (AMMNBA) in Camarines Norte. It is characterized by its distinctive spadix and is the 17th species of its kind discovered in the Philippines.

Corybas circinatus

A helmet orchid species discovered in Palawan. It is noted for its unique floral structure and is endemic to the region.

Nepenthes cabanae

A new species of pitcher plant found in the montane tropical rainforests of the Philippines. It is characterized by its unique pitcher morphology and is endemic to the region.

2021 Plant Discoveries

Dischidia argentii

A new species of Dischidia described in November 2021, named in honor of the late Dr. George Argent for his contribution to the flora of the Philippines. This species is known only from Mt. Kilang, Ilocos Norte.

Pinalia species

A new species of Pinalia (Orchidaceae) with bell-shaped flowers was discovered in the mossy forests of southern Mindanao. It is the first orchid species described from the Mount Busa Key Biodiversity Area.

2022 Plant Discoveries

Rafflesia banaoana

A new species of Rafflesia, known for its large, foul-smelling flowers, was discovered in the rainforest of Barangay Balbalasang, Kalinga. It is notable for its unique floral structure and is endemic to the region.

Begonia tanauanensis

An endemic begonia species found in Tanauan City, Batangas. It features unique leaf shapes and is closely related to other species in the region

2023 Plant Discoveries

Cryptocoryne zamboangaensis

A new species of water trumpet plant discovered in the Zamboanga Peninsula. It is recognized by its distinct purple spathe limb and is known only from the region.

Sonerila mapelo

A new species of Sonerila reported from the southwest Philippines. It is a terrestrial to epiphytic perennial herb, notable for its unique morphological characteristics

2024 Plant Discoveries

Aeschynanthus pentatrichomatus

A new species of 'lipstick vine' discovered in the Barangay Balbalasang rainforest. It is characterized by its white, purple-spotted flowers resembling a lipstick tube.

Bulbophyllum bokodense

An orchid species discovered in the Philippines, characterized by its unique floral structures and habitat preferences.

2025 Plant Discoveries

Begonia dorisiae

A new species of Begonia section Baryandra discovered on a rocky cliffside in the Philippines. It is named after broadcaster Doris Bigornia and is noted for its unique habitat and floral characteristics.

Etlingera lacerata

A new ginger species discovered in Mindanao Island. It is characterized by its distinctive floral structures and contributes to the diversity of the Zingiberaceae family in the region.
